Academic Statement

I endeavor to carry out research that integrates patient-centered clinical inquiry with reproducible scientific methodologies and advanced statistical analytics. My scholarly pursuit is dedicated to the cultivation of rigorous, data-driven frameworks designed to refine surgical decision-making and elevate the precision of trauma and sepsis care analytics.

By leveraging modern data science tools including R and Python, and aligning clinical insight with multidisciplinary collaboration, I am committed to supporting evidence-based transformation in medical research, healthcare systems evaluation, and population-level outcome optimization.

Through this synergy, I seek to drive the evidence-based transformation of healthcare systems and facilitate the optimization of population-level outcomes, ensuring that my professional contributions remain at the vanguard of medical innovation and systemic evaluation.
